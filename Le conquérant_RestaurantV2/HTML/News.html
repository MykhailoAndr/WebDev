Music Day in France

Fête de la Musique is an annual global music event that takes place on the summer solstice, June 21. The celebration is aimed at popularizing music among the population. On this day, free performances by music lovers and professional musicians take place in the streets, squares, parks and other places in thousands of cities.

On this wonderful day you will be able to listen to a live performance of one of the most famous singers Mylène Farmer from 20 pm to 23 pm
