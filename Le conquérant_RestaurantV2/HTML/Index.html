RESTAURANT LE CONQUÉRANT IN  PARIS

French gastronomy is at our table every day!

Led by talented Japanese chef Satoshi Horiuchi, this new gastronomic restaurant in Paris revisits French gastronomy with panache and modernity, while respecting small-scale producers and the seasons.

The spotlight is on gourmet French cuisine, meticulously prepared and upscale, with an explosion of flavors in every bite, all in a sophisticated, modern setting.

THE   MAP

For starters, white tuna served in a shellfish broth with green asparagus, or radishes sprinkled with African bottarga in a salicorne sauce.

Then a roasted Challans duckling with carrots and mustard duck jus, or a fillet of brill worked like sole meunière with white asparagus and garlic-smoked oil.

And don't forget the gourmet chocolate tartlet topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, or the strawberry blancmange.

OUR   TEAM

One of the world's best chefs at the helm Thibaut Ruggeri

Welcome to Le Conquérant! In our kitchen, we have selected specialists from all over the world, each of whom has come a long way to get to this kitchen and prove that they have the right to be here. For all of us, this work is the goal of our lives, which is why mistakes are not allowed. We are all united by a single goal - to give pleasure.

Thibaut Ruggeri is a recognized international chef and winner of the title "World's Best Chef". He has an exceptional talent in the culinary arts and is known for his aesthetics, refined taste and innovative approach to creating dishes.

Le Conquérant is a window into the world of his work. Discover the elegance and taste of his dishes.
